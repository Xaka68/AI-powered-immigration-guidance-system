Project work is governed by `.specify/memory/constitution.md`.

For this hackathon product, prioritize a polished, source-grounded migrant
journey over generic chatbot breadth. The model may route, translate, explain,
and personalize, but it must not invent bureaucratic steps. Multi-step flows
should use authored journey graphs, options-first UX, trusted source citations,
privacy minimization, and consent-based human handoff.
